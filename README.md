# No Fog CE

**No Fog CE** is a client-side addon for **Better Than Wolves CE 3.0.0** that intelligently pushes back the fog to maximize your visibility without breaking the game's natural horizon.

## ✨ Features

- **Maximized Clear Distance:** Pushes the fog start distance all the way back to 99% of your render distance. This gives you crystal clear vision for almost the entirety of your loaded world.
- **Natural Horizon Blending:** The fog rapidly thickens in the final 1% of your render distance. This creates a natural, tight "wall" that perfectly hides the void drop-off at the edge of the world.
- **Dynamic Adaptation:** The mod mathematically calculates the fog distance based on your current render distance settings, ensuring it always looks correct whether you are playing on Short or Extreme render distances.
- **Immersion Preserved:** Only affects standard overworld/land fog. It leaves blindness, lava, and underwater fog untouched so gameplay mechanics are preserved.

## 📥 Installation

1. Download the latest `.jar` file from the releases tab.
2. Drop it into your Better Than Wolves `mods` folder.
3. Launch the game and enjoy the view!
