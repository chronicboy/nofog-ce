## v1.0.0

### Features
- **Initial Release:** Optimized fog rendering for Better Than Wolves CE 3.0.0.
- **Maximized Clear Distance:** Pushes the fog start distance all the way back to 99% of your render distance for crystal clear vision of loaded chunks.
- **Natural Horizon Blending:** Implements a tight "fog wall" in the final 1% of the render distance to perfectly hide the void drop-off without obscuring loaded geometry.
- **Dynamic Adaptation:** Automatically scales based on your render distance settings (Short to Extreme).
- **Immersion Preserved:** Only affects land fog; blindness, lava, and underwater fog remain untouched.
